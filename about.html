What is Liyana ?

Our History

Liyana started its journey in November 20, 2010 in the interior fit out sector and became one of the forerunners in establishing leading industries such as Liyana Endeavor Ltd, With the belief that interior fit out works is not just about interior decoration work - but an Art, LEL goes beyond the traditional scopes of space management and integrates the best the country has to offer. Since then, LEL has emerged as the most reputed and fastest growing GENERAL INTERIORIOR COMPANY of the Nationwide.

A influential portfolio of the country’s most characteristic and selective organization, with an excellent reputation in the interior fit out market, and happy dealings that give our clients exclusive access to the ultimate in luxury residential and exquisite commercial spaces, all in located in the – Nationwide and as well as diplomatic Enclave.
